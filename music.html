Birdview
At the top of Rockefeller Center, I watched the sunset skyline of New York City stretch out beneath me. That moment of perspective — the city humming below, the sky on fire — became this piece.
The Mild Wind in Spring
Gathered in the fields brushed by a gentle spring breeze, the softly swaying blossoms are the wind's own gift.
Composed March 24, 2020
Looking Up
Late at night I gazed into the depths of the starry sky, as constellations lit up the heavens like a silver screen. This piece captures that flash of inspiration — and the feeling of waiting through the darkest hours for dawn.
Composed February 2020
A Day in Summer
A scorching summer day filled with hope and cool relief — moments of gentle warmth, moments of floral shade.
Composed April 4, 2020
Cloudy
