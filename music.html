My Original Compositions





Birdview

I was at Rockefeller Center in New York, and from the rooftop I watched the sunset skyline of the city.
That moment inspired me to write this piece, “Birdview.”





The Mild Wind in Spring

Gathered in the fields brushed by a gentle spring breeze,
the softly swaying blossoms are the wind’s own gift.
[Composed on March 24, 2020]





Looking Up

Late at night I gazed into the depths of the starry sky,
as constellations lit up the heavens like a silver screen.
This piece captures that flash of inspiration—and
the feeling of waiting through the darkest hours for dawn.
[Composed in February 2020]





A Day in Summer

A scorching summer day filled with hope and cool relief—
moments of gentle warmth, moments of floral shade.
[Composed on April 4, 2020]





Cloudy

All my feelings for overcast skies are condensed into
the rumbling thunder and drifting clouds of this piece.
[Composed on February 28, 2020]
