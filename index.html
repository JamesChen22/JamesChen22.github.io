About Me

I'm a student at Sage Hill School in Newport Coast, California with a 4.56 GPA. I've attended summer programs at NYU, UPenn, and Tufts — and I bring the same intensity to piano competitions as I do to robotics and AI development.

On the piano, I've performed at Carnegie Hall twice, earned multiple Grand Prix and Gold distinctions in international competitions, and composed 5 original pieces — one of which was acquired by a Chinese film studio.

In tech, I build AI-powered tools with Python, LangChain, and Java. I design robot subsystems with the FTC SDK, contribute CAD in Fusion 360, and research AI evaluation models with published academic work.
